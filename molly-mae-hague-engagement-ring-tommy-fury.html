Molly-Mae Hague shows off her huge engagement ring as she goes on luxury shopping spree

April 2024 · 3 minute read

MOLLY-MAE Hague showed off her huge diamond engagement ring as she enjoyed on luxury shopping spree.

The couple - who share daughter Bambi together - announced their engagement several days ago after the professional boxer surprised Molly-Mae with a romantic proposal.

Molly, 24, took a trip to Bicester Village in Oxfordshire on Sunday to enjoy some retail therapy.

The mum-of-one shared snaps of her carrying her designer shopping, including new purchases from Prada.

Molly looked effortlessly chic in a black vest top, some loose fitted matching trousers and a grey jumper draped around her shoulders.

The media personality shared a photo of her smiling as she visited a coffee shop on her outing.

But her massive diamond engagement ring did not go unnoticed.

Molly rested her hand on the table, giving fans a glimpse of the sparkly rock on her finger.

The Love Island star captioned it: "One week as a fiancé, still can't believe it"

Jewellery expert Laura Taylor says the ring is "perfect" for Molly-Mae and valued it at around the million pound mark.

Speaking to OK!, she said: "The main focus of the ring is a stunning five-carat oval diamond which looks to be near colourless with VVS1 clarity.

"These details show just how much care and attention to detail Tommy put into picking the perfect diamond to showcase his love for Molly."

Laura added: “In terms of the value of the ring, I would estimate that it is worth upwards of £600,000, potentially reaching a value of £1 million if Tommy had a role in designing the ring himself."

The couple announced they were getting engaged with a video of the moment Tommy popped the question.

In the clip posted to her Instagram account Molly became emotional as she walked into the romantic seaside setting to see Tommy suited and booted holding their baby daughter.

He handed over Bambi before dropping down on one knee and pulling the ring out.

The couple have been together for four years since meeting on Love Island.

Their engagement comes after it was reported the pair have their hearts set on a Disney themed wedding.

A source close to the pair claims the wheels are already in motion for their dream fairytale ceremony.

They told Heat: "Molly wants to book a castle somewhere in the UK, where they'd have a banquet from a Michelin-star chef, performances from famous musicians and a spectacular fireworks display.
