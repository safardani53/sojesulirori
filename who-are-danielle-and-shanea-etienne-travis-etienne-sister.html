Who Are Danielle And Shanea Etienne? Travis Etienne Sister

May 2024 · 4 minute read

Who is Travis Etienne’s sister? The NFL running back has two sisters: Danielle and Shanea. Although the two showed potential in athletics at high school, they referred to other careers later.

Etienne’s younger brother Trevor followed his footsteps into football & plays as a running back for the collegiate football team Florida Gators.

Trevor, for sure, sees the NFL dream to share the field with his brother Travis Etienne, who plays for the Jacksonville Jaguars.

The elder Etienne brother was the 25th overall pickup of the Jaguars in the 2021 NFL draft. There he joined his fellow college quarterback & fellow draft pick Trevor Lawrence.

However, Travis had to remain out of the 2021-22 season due to a Lisfranc injury in the pre-season game.

His NFL debut came in the 2022-23 season in the season-opener against Washington Commanders on September 11, 2022.

Etienne’s team Jacksonville Jaguars finished as the AFC South division leader & clinched the playoffs. Having won against the LA Chargers in the Wild Card round, they went to face the Kansas City Chiefs in the AFC Divisional round but lost.

No denying many fans expect a lot from the 24-year-old running back.

An alumnus of Clemson College, Travis led his college team to the 2018 ACC Championship title & was the two-time ACC Athlete of the Year awardee.

Travis Etienne Sister

Travis Etienne’s sisters, Danielle and Shanea, are athletes too. Both his sister ran tracks and played basketball for Jennings High School.

Both of his sisters were phenomenal, and they even won the state championships.

Shanea, the oldest sister of Travis, was born on June 30, 1991. She currently works as a registered nurse. She is also the mother of two daughters-Brook and Kollyns, who were born on May 30, 2012, and November 20, 2018, respectively.

Shanea is active on Instagram under the username @_s.lyons, where she actively posts pictures.

Moreover, his other sister Danielle celebrates her birthday on October 25. According to the source, she has two daughters and one son. Although her account is private, she is active on Instagram as @dannii_lyons. 

Additionally, Travis has a younger brother too. Born on July 9, Travis Etienne’s younger brother, Trevor Etienne, is an American football running back for the Florida Gators. He plays collegiate football at the University of Florida. 

Since his early days, his sisters have been by his side, encouraging and inspiring him. Further details regarding them are yet to be out.

Travis Etienne Parents

NFL Player Travis Etienne was born on January 26, 1999, to Travis Etienne Sr and Donnetta Etienne.

His parents raised him alongside his siblings in Jennings, Louisiana. His father works for a well-service company in the oil industry. Travis surprised his father on Father’s Day with Jags gear. 

Likewise, his mother, Donnetta, is a nurse by profession. Her real name is Donnetta Marie Lyons, and she is from Welsh, Louisiana.

Born on September 28, 1974, she is currently 49-year old now. Donnetta regularly updates her social media accounts with pictures of her kids to show her support for them.

Travis believes that his parents are the main reason for all his achievements. Both his parents are equally proud of the success Travis has achieved them. They show up to all his matches to encourage him.
