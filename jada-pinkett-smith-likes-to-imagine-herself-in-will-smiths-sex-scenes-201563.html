Jada Pinkett Smith Likes to Imagine Herself in Will Smith's Sex Scenes

July 2024 · 2 minute read

No green-eyed monster here! Not everyone would be okay with watching their spouse of almost 18 years get it on with another person — but not everyone is Jada Pinkett Smith, either. On Thursday, March 5, the Gotham actress stopped by Late Night With Seth Meyers, where she elaborated on some comments she'd made last month about enjoying husband Will Smith's sex scenes in movies.

"Here's the interesting part. When I'm looking at Will in those scenes, whoever's there is out of the way," the 43-year-old mom of two explained to Meyers. "I'm in the picture. I'm fantasizing." 

She went on to note that seeing her husband all loved up with, say, Margot Robbie in Focus, gives her an opportunity to see her husband in a different light — both literally and figuratively.

"When I'm with Will, I get to experience him physically, but very rarely do I get to see what he looks like when he's in the game," she quipped. "So I'm looking, and I'm like, 'Is that what he looks like?' And then I'm puttin' 20 on 10, 'cause I'm like, 'Well, if he's with me, it's gonna be extra!' But you know…it's sexy." 

It helps, of course, that Pinkett Smith is in the business herself and has therefore been in the position of having to cozy up to men who are not her husband. One prime example? Her role in Magic Mike XXL, which features lots of hunky, half-naked actors like Channing Tatum, Joe Manganiello, and Michael Strahan.

"I walk on my set for the first time…and my club is just chocolate tastiness. It's just 10, 11, 12 chocolate men getting greased down by the makeup department," she told Meyers of filming. "And literally, I walked on the set and my knee buckled."
