Jersey Shore's Nicole 'Snooki' Polizzi shuts down divorce rumors and insists wearing a wedding ring

July 2024 · 3 minute read

NICOLE “Snooki” Polizzi let fans know there is no reason to worry about her and husband Jionni LaValle  not wearing wedding rings. 

The Jersey Shore star responded to speculation from fans that she might be heading for a split after being spotted several times recently with no bling on her ring finger. 

After her followers continued to question her marital status, the TV personality set the record straight on her Instagram Stories.

In a black and white selfie video, Snooki, 33, said: “I just read some conspiracy theories about myself from fans noticing that I don’t wear my wedding rings anymore.

“I do this all day. I’m a mother of three. I don’t need to be wearing the ring around the house or to run and go get Starbucks. 

“It’s just not my thing,” she said in a serious tone. 

She went on to explain her reasoning: “I also don’t want to lose it. 

“I’m one of those a**holes that if I wore it 24/7, it would fall off in Walmart while I’m getting Gatorade, so it’s not happening.” 

The 4’8” “Nugget” said it hardly crosses her mind as her hubby does not wear his bands either. 

“Jionni hardly wears his ring either. Obviously I have my rings, they’re here - they’re in the bathroom. 

“I’ll wear it if we go on a date or we go to a family event or something cool, but other than that, I’m not wearing my ring b****es/

“Now you know, if I’m not wearing my ring, it’s okay,” the MTV star concluded. 

This is not the first time Nicole had to respond to rumors of her marriage crumbling due to not wearing the ring. 

In 2015, similar speculation began to buzz around and the Jersey girl shut down any talk she was headed for a divorce. 

Alongside a series of heart emoji’s, she tweeted: “FYI I don't wear my wedding rings because they're too big and I need to resize them so I won't lose them. I'm still happily married.”

Nicole and her man met at a club in her hometown of Seaside Heights, New Jersey.

Though she admitted she “hated” her future spouse at first, she thought their hookup would be a one-night-stand. 

However, the couple clearly hit it off and tied the knot in a lavish Jersey wedding in 2014. 

The couple welcomed their first child, Lorenzo, in August 2012 and Snooki gave birth to daughter Giovanna in September 2014. 

In May 2019, they welcomed their newest born, Angelo James LaValle. 

Snooki has been a main cast member of the MTV reality show and its revival spinoff, Jersey Shore: Family Vacation since 2009.
