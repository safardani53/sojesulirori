Who are Master P's children?

May 2024 · 5 minute read

MASTER P is an American rapper, record executive and entrepreneur who is widely considered one of the wealthiest hip hop artists in the US.

But away from success on the music scene, he has fathered a huge brood of nine children – sharing most with his ex-wife Sonya C.

Who are Master P's children?

Despite his fame, Master P has regularly commented on his nine children for whom he is believed to be close.

In May 2022, he cut a emotional figure as he revealed the death of his daughter, Tytyana Miller, at the age of 29.

In total, his big brood of children were born to three different mothers.

Here we take you through who Master’s kids are and what they are up to.

Romeo Miller

Master P’s eldest child is businessman Percy Romeo Miller.

Born on August 19, 1989, in New Orleans, Louisiana, mogul Miller was a childhood music star, who formerly went by the name Lil Romeo.

He first found fame signing under his father's No Limit Records.

He was known for his hit song My Baby and his hit 2001 album Lil' Romeo.

Miller has since founded the record labels Guttar Music, Take a Stand, The Next Generation and his current label No Limit Forever Records.

On screen, Miller also starred in the Nickelodeon show Romeo!, from 2003 to 2006.

The rapper then later appeared as a co-host of The Mix on Fox Soul.

In February 2022, he announced the birth of his first daughter with girlfriend Drew Sangster.

Vercy Miller

Vercy Miller, also known as Young V, was born on July 26, 1991.

Like his father, he is an actor and has appeared in various films including The Team, Decisions and I Got The Hook Up 2.

On the music scene, Vercy is also believed be a part time producer and DJ.

He is active on Instagram where, at the time of writing, he has amassed 16.7k followers.

Veno Miller

Veno Miller is not actually Master P's biological son.

Master reportedly adopted Veno after his brother, Kevin Miller, was killed in a robbery in 1990, according to Distractify.

His date of birth is July 16, 1991, so he's ten days older than his brother - and cousin - Vercy.

Miller has also embarked on a career and appeared on TV in shows such as Victorious and LaLa Land.

As of 2022, he is best known for his role in the 2021 film Malibu Horror Story.

Tytyana Miller

Tytyana Miller, 29, was the daughter of Master P and Sonya C who tragically passed away in May 2022.

Born on June 15, 1992, she was thrust into the limelight from an early age.

She cameoed in several films as an actress including her role in Carnetta Jones’s 2010 film A Mother's Choice.

Miller also modelled on Instagram where she would regularly pose in photoshoots for fashion designers and cosmetic brands such as LaQuan Smith.

Most notably, however, she appeared alongside her father and her brother Romeo in the WE TV series Growing Up Hip Hop which documented her drink and drug addiction.

Paying tribute upon her death, Master P said: “Our family is dealing with an overwhelming grief for the loss of my daughter Tytyana.

“Mental illness & substance abuse is a real issue that we can’t be afraid to talk about. With God, we will get through this. #MyAngel.”

Inty Miller

Intylyana Miller, known as Inty and Princess Inty, is an emerging R&B artist who has an estimated net worth of $500,000.

She was born on June 25, 1993.

In 2017, she released her first song titled That Real Love, followed by a second single called You Know I See You.

Her other songs include For Better Or Worse and Whatever It Takes.

Like other members of the family, she has also starred in film A Mother’s Choice.

In 2019, she played a character named Makala in I Got The Hook Up 2.

Cymphonique Miller

Cymphonique Miller was born in 1996.

Her father is Master P but her mother has never been publicly revealed.

She is a singer and an actress, who had guest roles on Big Time Rush, Phineas And Ferb, How To Rock, and Winx Club.

Her last film credit to date was in the 2020 romance movie, Never And Again.

Itali Miller

Itali was born on May 6, 1999, as Master’s seventh child and Sonya's sixth.

The 20-year-old actress has appeared in movies like A Mother's Choice and I Got The Hook Up 2.

She also appeared in the 2010 season of the Flip The Script Kids Reality Special.

As of 2022, she is an active Instagram user who has over 28,000 followers.

Her profile is captioned: “A young woman who possesses the quality 'It'; absolute attraction.”

Pictured in a post with her famous father in 2021, she said: “Thank you for showing me God, Family, Love, and Integrity. You are the light of my life. Love you Daddy.”

Hercy Miller

Hercy, born on March 28, 2002, is an up-and-coming basketball player who is currently playing for the Xavier University’s Men’s basketball team.

He transferred from Tennessee State in 2022, after reportedly landing a $2million Name, Image and Likeness deal, according to the Nashville Tennessean.

Speaking in January, head coach Travis Steele confirmed that Miller had joined the squad.

He said: "He (Hercy) transferred in from Tennessee State where he was playing this year as a freshman, got injured and then I think he just decided he wanted to try to experience something different in college basketball.

“He's a great young man, comes from a really good family."

Mercy Miller

Mercy Miller is the youngest of the nine children, born on June 4, 2003.

Like his elder brother Hercy, Mercy is also loves the game of basketball.

Mercy transferred from Minnehaha Academy to Oak Hill Academy in January 2022.
