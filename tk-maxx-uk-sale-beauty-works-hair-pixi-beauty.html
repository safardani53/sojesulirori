February 2024 · 2 minute read

BEAUTY fanatics are racing to TK Maxx as some of their favourite products have been slashed in its clearance sale. 

Products from Pixi by Petra, Beauty Works and designer skincare are less than a fiver - despite still being sold for full-price elsewhere. 

The £16 Pixi by Petra Pat Away Concealing Base has been slashed to £3, but is still on sale for £16 everywhere else. 

The paraben-free product contains an elastic and long-wearing formula with Antioxidant protection.

The applicator is sized like a finger with extra flocking for added softness when blending in - and yes, it’s buildable for a full coverage. 

The shades available in the TK Maxx clearance sale are Mocha and Espresso.

Full-size bottles of Molly-Mae's beloved Beauty Works’ After Sun Colour Lockdown Shampoo are on sale for £4 in the sale too. 

Still on sale elsewhere for £11.99, the formula is enriched with Argan and Macadamia Oil to stop your dye from fading. 

The shampoo was designed with holidays in mind and contains a UV guard to protect your hair from drying out in the sun. 

You can use it before going in the sun or after a colour treatment to close down cuticles.

TK Maxx has also slashed the price of Shiseido eye-shadow pots from £27 to £6, while a number of War Paint products are also £6. 

Rodial’s Suede Lips Crayon in Black Caramel has been dropped by nearly 80% to £5 from £24. 

Indie Lee fans can pick up a host of shower gels for £4, down from £23 - including the Sleep, Destress and Energize body washes. 

Phytorelax Laboratories Coconut and Anti Frizz Shampoos are down by 60% to £4 from £10. 

Boots has similarly slashed the price of some designer beauty items.

Fabulous reported yesterday that shoppers can nab the likes of YSL, NARS and Fenty Beauty for a fraction of the retail price. 

Beauty fans are also scrambling to Sainsbury’s to pick up goodies slashed to pennies.

Among these are false eyelash sets for 50p, claw clips for 80p and nail care products starting at 60p.
