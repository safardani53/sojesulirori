You People Is Set To Released On Netflix

July 2024 · 3 minute read

You People, Jonah Hill’s next Netflix film, is planned to launch on Netflix on Friday, January 27, 2023, at 3 a.m. ET. The film is about a couple and their family who face several obstacles in their personal lives as they deal with cultural clashes and other concerns related to contemporary living. Jonah Hill is the lead, with Eddie Murphy and several others filling significant supporting parts. Kenya Barris directed You People and co-wrote the screenplay with Hill.

You People on Netflix: Trailer highlights the film’s cheerful tone

On January 5, 2023, Netflix published the official trailer for You People, which features various humorous scenes from the film. The plot’s central conflict is established right away: protagonist Ezra Cohen struggles with his relationships and is dissatisfied that he has never found a woman who understands him. Although the trailer is slightly longer than three minutes, it contains no spoilers and establishes the film’s easygoing, humorous tone, which fans of buddy comedies will undoubtedly like. According to Netflix’s Tudum, the narrative of the film is as follows:

“Kenya Barris’ directorial debut, co-written with actor Jonah Hill, puts an interfaith, cultural clash spin on the romantic rite of passage. The film follows Ezra and Amira as they traverse the perilous seas that connect family and passion.”

According to the teaser and description, audiences can anticipate a truly fun, chaotic buddy comedy that addresses a variety of contemporary subjects such as cultural tensions, family, love, and modern-day relationships, among others. The film was released in select theaters on January 20, 2023, to wonderful reviews from audiences and reviewers alike, who praised the film’s snappy writing, performances by the actors, and comedy, among other things.

More information about the You People cast and crew

Jonah Hill plays Ezra Cohen in You People, who is unhappy and disillusioned that he hasn’t had a steady relationship and is seeking a lady that understands him. Hill looks fantastic in the teaser, showcasing his trademark sense of humor that perfectly establishes the tone of the picture. Fans may look forward to a remarkable performance from the celebrity. In addition to You People, Hill has appeared in Martin Scorsese’s The Wolf of Wall Street, Superbad, and This Is the End, to mention a few. Mid90s and Stutz are among his directing credits.

Lauren London, who plays Amira Mohammed, co-stars with Hill in another important role. London portrays Hill’s love interest in the film and looks fantastic in this hilarious persona. They also have fantastic on-screen chemistry. Other significant film and television acting credits for Lauren London include Madea’s Big Happy Family, Games People Play, and many more. The supporting cast also includes Eddie Murphy, Nia Long, David Duchovny, and Julia Louis-Dreyfus, among many others. Kenya Barris, the creator of Black-ish, directs the film. You People is set to premiere on Netflix on Friday, January 27, 2023, at 3 a.m. ET.
