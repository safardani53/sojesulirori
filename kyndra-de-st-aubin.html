Kyndra De St. Aubin Biography

April 2024 · 1 minute read

Kyndra graduated from the University of Minnesota and started her career with ESPN radio covering all sports in the Milwaukee area as a beat reporter for the Bucks (NBA), Brewers (MLB), Packers (NFL), and all UW Badger sports. She was also the afternoon SportsCenter anchor. She is a talented sports journalist who gained precious experience at a very young age.

 Later Kyandra moved to Arizona and joined Arizona sports 98.7 FM covering the Diamondbacks (MLB), Suns (NBA), Cardinals (NFL), Coyotes (NHL) and ASU soccer. She was also a pre-game host and sideline reporter for Grand Canyon University men’s basketball and baseball on Cox sports.

Kyndra is a married woman and lives with her husband Bobby and beautiful daughter Adelynne in bay Area, Los Gatos, CA. Kyndra earns a beautiful amount of salary from her sports journalism career. Though she has not outed her net worth, we can assume it to be in millions after seeing several of ESPN co-workers working in the same position for the sports network. Further, She is very active on social media sites such as Facebook and twitter and has got many followers.
