Who Is LaMarcus Aldridge?

LaMarcus Aldridge is an American free-agent basketball player who last played for the Brooklyn Nets of the National Basketball Association(NBA).

Aldridge was born on July 19, 1985, to Marvin and Georgia Aldridge. He grew up with his brother LaVontae Aldridge who guided him on the importance of basketball fundamentals.

The free agent has been active in basketball since his high school days. The Chicago Bulls selected Aldridge in the 2006 NBA Draft, only to hand rights to Portland Blazers.

Aldridge has been a professional NBA player for 15 years and has played for several clubs. He also had some career highlights and achievements with such long playing history.
