June 2024 · 2 minute read

DO you dream about tucking into the perfect doner kebab?

A woman has revealed how you only need two ingredients and a slow cooker to make your vision a reality within hours.

TikTok user @ssiwtaj uploaded a video with the caption “slow cooker doner kebab so easy”, and it has racked up hundreds of likes.

She showed how you simply need to pick up some lamb mince, and a sachet of Colman’s Big Night In Doner Kebab flavouring.

All you do is add the sachet onto the mince meat and mix it together.

Then you need to make the ingredients into a sausage shape and wrap it in foil.

More on slow cookers

She added: “Slow cooker on low for 7 to 8 hours.”

In the comments she explained that she added a small amount of water to stop the foil from sticking to the bottom of the slow cooker.

In the clip, she then opened the foil to reveal the meat and claimed it was “yum yum.”

She then sliced the meat into long slithers and served it up with wedges, sauce and some vegetables.
