Reese Witherspoon and More Best-Dressed Stars at Hollywood Film Awards

February 2024 · 3 minute read

Red carpet ready! Stars like Reese Witherspoon and Jenna Dewan Tatum brought their A-game while attending the 2014 Hollywood Film Awards at the Palladium on Friday, Nov. 14. Some sparkled, some covered up, and one in particular had an accidental nip slip!

Check out Us Weekly’s best-dressed (in no particular order!):

Reese Witherspoon
The Wild actress presented the Breakthrough Director Award to Jean-Marc Vallee while wearing a black and red J. Mendel dress. She accessorized with Harry Winston jewelry and wore minimal makeup.

Jenna Dewan Tatum
His hot plus one! The former Witches of East End star supported her husband Channing Tatum and his new film Foxcatcher. She showed off her toned abs in a shimmering grey Reem Acra gown and Brian Atwood heels.

Keira Knightley
The English actress looked elegant in a white long-sleeve Giambatista Valli dress and Harry Winston jewelry while accepting the award for Best Supporting Actress for her work in The Imitation Game.

Kristen Stewart
Wardrobe malfunction! The Twilight alum looked edgy in a knee-length white dress with a sleeveless mesh top. Though beautiful, the loose-fitting top sagged down while she presented on stage, which resulted in an unfortunate nip slip.

Angelina Jolie
No introduction necessary! The Unbroken actress wore a black Atelier Versace gown while walking her first red carpet since marrying Brad Pitt back in August.

Queen Latifah
The host of the evening was golden in a bronze-colored Badgley Mischka dress. “I’m just happy to be a part of it, happy to be the one to kick it off,” she told Entertainment Weekly of the gig.

Jennifer Lopez
Sizzling! The “I Luh Ya Papi” singer messed up her lines on stage — but looked great doing it! She wore a black and white Zuhair Murad dress, Brian Atwood heels, and Norman Silverman jewelry.

Shailene Woodley
The Divergent actress wore a plunging neckline Valentino gown while accepting the Breakthrough Performance Award for her work in the tearjerker The Fault in Our Stars.

Julianne Moore
Red hot! The star wore a white long-sleeve Balenciaga dress and Harry Winston earrings for the special night out. Stewart presented her with the Best Actress Award for her performance in Still Alice.

Hilary Swank
The Homesman actress wore a tight-fitting Oakwell Couture dress and Christian Louboutin pumps on the red carpet. She also squeezed in some time to pose with her former P.S. I Love You costar Gerard Butler.
