Kanye West 'whisks model Irina Shayk away on romantic trip to France for his birthday' amid divorce

August 2024 · 3 minute read

KANYE West was reportedly spotted with Irina Shayk on a romantic getaway in France for his 44th birthday.

The Yeezy mogul and Russian supermodel, 35, hung out at a luxury hotel in Provence in the south of France, according to TMZ.

The duo - who The Sun first revealed were linked on Instagram gossip site Deux Moi last month - seemed comfortable in each other's presence as they strolled around the grounds of a boutique hotel, the outlet reported.

According to the photos shared by DailyMail.com, the Victoria's Secret model was sporting a simple outfit made up of a white tee that showed off her toned stomach and white pants.

She also kept a hold of her mask as she walked alongside the rapper.

Kanye, on the other hand, smiled and laughed as he walked behind her in a dark outfit complimented with a gold chain.

Kanye, 44, and Irina, 35, reportedly chatted and laughed and "looked very much like a couple," as TMZ reported.

Despite spending most of their time on their own, the pair was joined by a group of Kanye's team as they took in the rural sights.

Even though the two are said to be romantically involved, they kept their hands to themselves during their stroll.

Kanye and Irina - who has a daughter with ex Bradley Cooper - were first romantically linked late last month by gossip site Deux Moi, who claimed they were "dating."

Sources exclusively told The Sun at the time that Kanye's ex Kim Kardashian was not "upset or bothered" by the rumors.

“She's not bothered at all about it, no one in her circle appears to care or believe it," an insider said.

The source added: "Even if it is true, I doubt she'll even be upset at this point."

Irina's connection to Kanye goes as far back as 2010, when she appeared in the music video for his song Power.

In the clip, the mother-of-one wears a revealing gold outfit as she kneels beside Kanye.

She also walked the runway for Kanye's Fall/Winter fashion collection at Paris Fashion Week in 2012.

More recently, Irina wore a $200 DMX tribute T-shirt that Kanye designed with fashion house Balenciaga in April.

She rocked the T-shirt while on a walk in New York with Lea - the four-year-old daughter she shares with ex Bradley.

Irina and A Star Is Born actor Bradley, 45, split in June 2019 after four years of dating and they share custody of Lea.

As for Kanye, Kim filed for divorce in February after months of speculation that their six-year marriage was over.

They have four children together - North, seven, Saint, five, Chicago, three, and Psalm, two.

Sources exclusively told The Sun this week that Kim hopes the rapper moves on first and starts dating someone before she does.

An insider tells us: "It's a tricky situation - I think Kim is hoping that Kanye dates first. She needs to know how he'll react to her dating."
