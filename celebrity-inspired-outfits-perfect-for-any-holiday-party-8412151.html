Celebrity-Inspired Outfits Perfect for Any Holiday Party

June 2024 Â· 4 minute read

The festive season means plenty of celebrations and a need for party outfits. Looking for some inspiration? We have six celebrity-inspired looks perfect for any holiday plans you may have. From a fancy night of cocktails or an office party to just a friend's dinner party, we have the perfect stylish (and affordable!) look for you. Get inspired and shop these stars' outfits for less and enjoy your holidays in style.

Gabrielle Union

Looking for a cool and casual party look? Take a note from Gabrielle Union's unexpected denim and sequins combo. Dress up your favorite pair of wide-leg jeans with a sparkly top and cool accessories, like a metallic clutch, statement rhinestone earrings and embellished pumps. A front tuck adds an extra cool factor to the trendy look.

Celeb Inspo: Gabrielle Union in Kate Spade New York.

Mandy Moore

Mandy Moore proves how versatile a velvet dress can be. Style a jewel-toned velvet midi with a pair of tan suede boots for a more daytime look or go for a more classic dressed-up look by simply swapping the footwear to a black or gold heel. But don't get us wrong, we love the idea of the knee-high style for an evening look as well! For extra warmth, add a black or ivory blazer for indoors or a black moto jacket or faux fur coat depending how dressy (or warm!) you want to be.

Celeb Inspo: Mandy Moore in an Ulla Johnson Dress with Alexandre Birman boots.

Buy It! Zara Dress, $139; zara.com, Anthropologie Earrings, $44; anthropologie.com, Charles & Keith Bag, $76; charleskeith.com, Steve Madden Boots, $169; stevemadden.com

Allison Williams

Daytime sequins? Yes, please! Allison Williams nails the look. A cream oversize knit worn untucked dresses down the sparkly silver midi skirt and creates a chic and elevated monochromatic look perfect for the office or a classy cocktail night out with the girls. Simple nude pumps and a silver crystal drop earring and metallic bag complete the polished ensemble.

Celeb Inspo: Allison Williams in a cream sweater and sequin skirt.

Danai Gurira

A black jumpsuit is a holiday outfit no brainer. Look for one with a wide-leg for an of-the-moment look, like Danai Gurira's Max Mara style. A metallic blazer, statement rhinestone earrings and a velvet bag add the perfect amount of glam.

Celeb Inspo: Danai Gurira in Max Mara.

Hilary Duff

Need a comfy, but stylish look? Try a trendy silky wide-leg pant in a jewel-tone emerald green hue paired with a turtleneck sweater in the coordinating color like Hilary Duff. You'll feel like you're wearing pajamas, but look as stylish as a star.

Jennifer Garner
