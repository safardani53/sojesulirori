  • The MLB icon split with his fitness influencer girlfriend Kathryne Padget in September 2022, following his breakup with Jennifer Lopez in April 2021.

Alex Rodriguez has always been known as a bit of a ladies’ man. However, there was a good chunk of time he was strictly known as Jennifer Lopez’ boyfriend, even fiance at one point. Their red-hot romance ended in April 2021, with J.Lo making up for lost time with her new husband Ben Affleck. Meanwhile, A.Rod was back on the scene, single and ready to mingle, and was rumored to quickly find a new love interest in Kathryne Padget, a 25-year-old fitness influencer.

The MLB icon’s relationship with Katheryne was short-lived though and the duo reportedly split up in September 2020. Again, A.Rod brushed himself off, got back on the saddle and was soon snapped with another gorgeous blonde, whom the DailyMail confirmed as Jaclyn Cordeiro. Find out all about the new lady in the baseball champ’s life, below!

Where is Jaclyn from?

Jaclyn is a Canadian-based fitness influencer with over 66k followers on her Instagram, where she lists herself as a “Lifestyle Transformation Specialist/RN.” On her Facebook page, she says she hails from Ontario, went to St. Thomas of Villanova Catholic Secondary School and studied at the University of Windsor.

The workout guru grew up with two brothers and tried sports such as soccer, Taekwondo and boxing in childhood, per Oxygen.

She ‘Fell In Love’ With Wrestling While Studying To Be A Nurse

She would eventually earn her bachelor’s degree in science and honors in nursing from the University of Windsor, according to the outlet. While tackling her studies, she was turned on to something else that would influence her life forever.

“[When] I ventured into the world of weightlifting, I immediately fell in love,” Jacklyn told Oxygen. “This newfound love helped me through the stress of nursing school and kept me focused on my life goals.”

Jaclyn Is The Proud Mom Of Two Daughters

Just like ARod, Jaclyn has two daughters, whom she calls Bella and Savanah. “I am currently a single mother with two beautiful girls ages 8 and 9 years old,” she told Status in May 2021. “My life’s goal is to raise strong, independent, kind daughters.”

Five Months After Giving Birth, She Competed As An Athlete

Jaclyn competed in the Canadian Physique Alliance competition just five months after giving birth to her first child, per Oxygen. “I figured there was no better way to get my ‘pre-baby’ body back,” she said. “I decided to compete again and continued on to compete three more times in the natural division as a bikini fitness CPA plant-based athlete.”

She also told Status, “I knew what it took to get into competition-ready shape, so I competed 5 months to the day after giving birth to my first baby and won 2nd place in my local OPA. Then I found out I was pregnant with my second child, just 2 months after competing. I decided to yet again, compete 5 months after having my second daughter. I placed 4th in the Bikini category.”

Jaclyn Is A Plant-Based Trainer

Jaclyn believes a diet that is plant-based is the best way to go for overall health. “My diet consists of a plant-based, I transitioned from animal-based to plant-based about 6 years ago,” she told Status in 2021. “I cannot express enough the benefits for my body both physically and the overall mental clarity and function.
