Ex on the beach star Jemma Lucy strips off to her underwear and flashes tattoos with semi-naked Ross

September 2024 · 4 minute read

JEMMA Lucy has teamed up with fellow Ex On The Beach star Ross Worswick to showcase a new range of underwear.

Both TV stars have stripped down to their smalls for a shoot for Saint Couture underwear - and confidently pose in front of the cameras as they showcase their toned bodies.

Heavily tattooed Jemma, 28, displays almost all of her body art as she takes the spotlight in a mainly white pair of bra and knickers.

While fellow reality star Ross, 27, proves he's spent plenty of time at the gym as he flexes his muscles and shows off his abs in a white pair of boxers.

Teaming up, Jemma and Ross show a range of designs including grey boxers, cammo green boxers, red bra and knickers, and a pink bra.

Jemma has featured on Series three and five of Ex on the Beach while Ross appears in the current sixth series of the hit MTV show as well as previously appearing in series one.

Fans of the MTV stars will no doubt be used to seeing them both wearing very little as they frequently post saucy selfies on social media.

And indeed on their hit reality show, viewers have got to know them intimately - not least because co-stars have freely discussed their sex lives on air.

Such raunchy talk has not gone unnoticed by fellow former reality star Jodie Marsh who recently incurred the wrath of Jemma after her comments about reality stars acting like porn stars stirred controversy.

The glamour model, 38, has annoyed scores of small screen heroes after comparing them to “unpaid porn stars”, and declaring that the public want “classy” celebrities – not people having casual sex on TV.

Last night Jemma – who found fame on Ex On The Beach – hit back at Jodie, hours before she was seen on the balcony of her new flat not wearing any trousers.

Ranting on Snapchat, she said: “Hang on a minute, they want something classy?

“Oh let’s all watch something classy like Jodie Marsh. Look at the state of it! That is not class.

“How can you slag off reality TV when you come from it?

“Is this classy? No mate, so f*****g do one you s**g.”

Jodie first found fame on ITV docusoap Essex Wives back in 2002.

At the time she was working as a stripper in Stringfellows, but her popularity saw her launch her own reality career off the back of it.

As well as starring on Celebrity Big Brother, Jodie also appeared in Trust Me I’m A Holiday Rep, Snog Marry Avoid, London Ink, Come Dine With Me, Fool Around… with Jodie Marsh and Back To Reality.

Jemma added: “It’s actually upset me that I’ve seen that today because I used to like Jodie Marsh.

“But after seeing that, how thick are you? TV isn’t about class.

“You don’t watch a TV show full of classy people walking around holding their tea-cups.

“Nah, you want to watch sex, fighting, swearing – you want to watch entertainment.”

She even looked for a husband on MTV’s Totally Jodie Marsh: Who’ll Take Her Up the Aisle? – although it later turned out she already knew the man she picked to wed, throwing the show in to chaos.

Jemma referenced it, saying: “Oh hang on, let me make a TV show about trying to find a husband and then it failing. Nice one, mate.”

Jemma told her fans that she thinks a lot of people have short memories in the showbiz industry.

She added: “I just hate when people aren’t grounded and forgot where they came from and forget what show has made them who they are.

“You should never forget your roots. Ex On The Beach!”

It wasn’t long before Jodie heard about Jemma’s comments and took to Twitter to challenge her.

Jodie posted: “Erm this is a girl who was bragging about having the same bikini as me and trying to be my mate not so long ago.”

Jemma said: “Yeah cos I used to like u a lot and look up to u ! Not anymore.”

Jodie really wasn’t bothered, ending the discussion with, “So you don’t like me because I said people who have sex on tv will regret it one day. Ok babe.”
