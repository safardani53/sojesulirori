Julian Draxler celebrates 24th birthday with incredible PSG cake

August 2024 · 2 minute read

JULIAN DRAXLER showed no signs of wanting to leave Paris Saint-Germain as he posed for a birthday snap.

The Germany international has been linked with a move away from PSG, with Arsenal and Liverpool both reportedly keen.

But Draxler, who is 24 today, posted a picture on Instagram of him stood next to an incredible PSG-themed birthday cake.

In the extravagant Paris love-in, a large model of the Eiffel Tower is one of two he had visible in the background.

There were also mini Eiffel Towers in a picture frame as well as on the side of his three-tier cake.

Draxler shared the image, with the caption: "Thank you for all the birthday wishes and messages! You made it even more special."

The 37-cap star was one of few Germany players to impress at Euro 2016, before he secured a £30million move from Wolfsburg last December.

But he started just 12 Ligue 1 games for the minted Parisian club in the second half of the campaign.

Draxler missed the opening two league games this season and was only brought off the bench in the final ten minutes of PSG's following two.

But has started the last two against Lyon and Metz and provided an assist in the 5-0 Champions League romp at Celtic.

With Neymar, Kylian Mbappe and Edinson Cavani available to boss Unai Emery, Draxler faces a fight to start in a three-man attack.

But Draxler, Mbappe and Neymar played behind Cavani in the 2-0 win over Lyon, with the German surely hoping Emery sticks with that set-up.
