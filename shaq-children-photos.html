Shaquille O’Neal is not only a 7’1-tall NBA Champion, a best-selling musician, a movie star and a pop culture icon, but he’s also a father of six. However, the couple filed for divorce in November 2009. The petition from Shaq’s lawyer read in part, “The marriage between the parties is irretrievably broken.”

Their daughter Taahirah O’Neal was born in July 1996 and now works with charities and philanthropic causes in Atlanta, Georgia. He also has a son, Myles O’Neal, who was born to Shaunie Nelson from a previous relationship. Despite splitting from his ex-wife, Myles maintains a close relationship with Shaq.
