THE ARREST

As The Sun exclusively revealed, June’s boyfriend was hit with the arrest warrant after he admitted to doing party drugs while in rehab, and then failing to appear for his meetings with his Alabama probation officer in October. 

Justin skipped out on his probation officer meetings in Alabama just days after he was seen showing off his new tattoo in Atlanta.    

The warrant for Justin's arrest alleged he was kicked out of rehab in Alabama for suspected drug abuse in September.   

He allegedly admitted to his probation officer that he used “water,” which is otherwise known as party drug GHB.   

From the warrant report, the probation officer appeared willing to work with him through the alleged slip-up and allowed him to be transferred from Autauga County CCP to Lauderdale County CCP, where he maintained employment and had a place to live.   

He reported to Lauderdale probation as required in September, but failed to appear for his probation meetings on October 21, 22 and 25, with the warrant for his arrest being issued on October 26.   

Justin was originally arrested for possession of marijuana and drug paraphernalia, as well as burglary at a dwelling in the third degree and theft of property in November of 2019. 

He pleaded guilty to the latter two charges.
