GOING STRONG?

While the Jersey Shore cast stopped partying at Karma long ago, the show is still airing in a new iteration.

The first season of Jersey Shore: Family Vacation aired in 2018.

It has continued on for six seasons, capturing various life changes for its cast members.

When the show began, it starred Nicole "Snooki" Polizzi, Jenni "JWoww" Farley, DJ Pauly D, Mike "The Situation" Sorrentino, Vinny Guadagnino, Deena Nicole Cortese, Angelina Pivarnick and Ronnie Ortiz-Magro.

As the show continued airing, Sammi "Sweetheart" Giancola signed on to appear on the show.

Early this month, MTV revealed the fate of the show amid concern from fans that Family Vacation was headed for cancellation.

The reality show was renewed for Season 7.

The new season will debut in 2024, according to Deadline.

The news came after the second half of Season 6 brought in some of the best ratings the network had seen in years.

Paramount Global announced that the show brought in over 18M engagements.

That number marked a season-over-season growth of 128 percent for the network.

When the news was handed down, there were still a few episodes of Season 6 left.
