When one’s dream comes true, we shed tears of joy and leap on happiness. The same goes for Amanda; with a series of games canceled, she was finally called up to start in the big leagues.

Amanda Ribas stated while recalling that day,

Right then, Ribas had her debut match with Juliana Lima at The Ultimate Fighter 25 Finale on July 7, 2017; however, USADA removed her from the match.
