May 2024 · 3 minute read

WHEEL of Fortune fans were left grappling with a WWE star's decision to wear a crop top on Friday's episode.

Austin Theory and his teammate may not have won the weeklong special but his revealing look had Twitter in a headlock.

Wheel of Fortune hit the mat this week with a special WWE tournament.

Each night, three regular players were paired with pro wrestlers, and the top three highest-scoring teams went head-to-head on Friday.

Austin Theory and Ray advanced having won on Tuesday.

The charismatic Big E. (who during his first appearance flirted with Vanna White) and Ben advanced on Wednesday.

Yet even Pat Sajak, 76, could not ignore Austin's outfit.

The star wore a halter top that cut off at his torso and displayed his entire abs, and had a frilled schoolboy-style collar to boot.

Pat said: “I have to ask you. Did you have to work as hard as I did to get the abs in that kind of shape?"

WWE SPECIAL ENDS WITH RARE FAIL

Grant and Natalya won out with an impressive $54K - emerging as the victors of the entire week.

That said, they lost the bonus round and no one won it all week, which was a first for the game show- fans claimed.

They chose “Events” as their bonus round category and couldn't guess “Joyous Occasions” - they lost also out on a Ford for a second time this week, which hurt to watch.

Viewers were also disappointed as no bonus round victories in a week hasn't happened before.
