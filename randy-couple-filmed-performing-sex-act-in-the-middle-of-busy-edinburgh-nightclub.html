Randy couple filmed performing sex act in the middle of busy Edinburgh nightclub

July 2024 · 2 minute read

A RAUNCHY couple performed an explicit sex act in full view of a busy nightclub on Halloween.

The pair were filmed carrying out their X-rated tryst at Edinburgh club ATIK.

Shocked club goers can be seen sitting just inches away from the dirty duo as they snogged while partaking in the sex act.

A spokesperson from ATIK Edinburgh said: "While we encourage our customers to enjoy their night, this type of behaviour is clearly unacceptable and we encourage all customers to report any inappropriate behaviour to our team so appropriate action can be taken."

The footage has been widely shared across social media and punters have been quick to slate the pair.
