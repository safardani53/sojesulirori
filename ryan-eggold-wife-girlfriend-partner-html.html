Ryan Eggold Bio: Age, Parents, Ethnicity

The acting was in the actor’s feat as he was actively involved in many school theater performances before he graduated from Santa Margarita Catholic High School in 2002. Later, he enrolled in theater arts department in the University of Southern California from where he graduated in 2006.
