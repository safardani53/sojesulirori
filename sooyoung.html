Sooyoung is of Asian ancestry, while her zodiac sign is. Sooyoung was born as the youngest daughter of Moon Sohee and Choi Jungnam. She grew up in Gwangju alongside her elder sister, Choi Soo Jin, who is also an actress and singer. As a child, she looked up to her sister, who inspired her to pursue a career as a singer.
