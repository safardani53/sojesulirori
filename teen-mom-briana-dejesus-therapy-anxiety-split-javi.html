BRIANA DeJesus revealed she's seeing a therapist to help her cope with "stress and anxiety" after her split from fiance Javi Gonzalez.

The Teen Mom 2 star, 27, opened up about her counselling and said it's the "best thing" she's ever done.

Briana wrote on her Instagram Stories: "I've been seeing a therapist. It really has helped me in such a small amount of time."

The mother-of-two shared a screenshot of the sorts of sessions she can join on a therapist app she is using.

One of the sessions is titled: "Stress and anxiety in parenthood."

Briana revealed she gets given homework on "coping skills for anxiety" and "self-care tips."

SELF-IMPROVEMENT

"So when I text it or write it down it just makes more sense. Best thing I ever did."
