Rapper Meek Mill welcomes a son with girlfriend Milan Harris on his 33rd birthday The US Sun

August 2024 · 2 minute read

RAPPER Meek Mill welcomed a son with his girlfriend, Milan Harris, on Wednesday.

The star was thrilled that the baby boy was born on his 33rd birthday.

The Philadelphia rapper - whose real name is Robert Rihmeek Williams - announced on Twitter that he had become a father for the third time.

He tweeted: "Milano dropped me off a king on my birthday! #thebestgift"

His announcement was met with congratulatory tweets from fans and friends.

Despite having just given birth, Milan - who is also known as Milano Di Rouge - took to Instagram to wish her boyfriend a happy birthday.

Along with a series of photos, she wrote: "Happy Birthday Love @meekmill.

"I hope this year is filled with more blessings, lots of love, peace & prosperity.

"I’m beyond proud of the man you’ve become. You motivate and inspire me in so many ways."

The couple has yet to share any photos of their new addition.

Milan and Meek have apparently been dating for more than a year, but only confirmed the news of their relationship and her pregnancy in February.

In a now-deleted tweet, the rapper wrote: "My girl is with me pregnant watching me tweet about my ex is very clownish... Ima exit.”

Meek's famous ex is rapper Nicki Minaj, and the two have been involved in a longtime feud.

Though they split in 2016, the Super Bass rapper, 37, claimed he “spit on” his own sister earlier this year.

Meek hit back by falling her brother a "rapist," as just weeks before, Jelani Maraj was sentenced to 25 years in prison for sexually assaulting an 11-year-old girl.
