A Look Into Miley Cyrus and Liam Hemsworth Relationship

June 2024 · 3 minute read

How long did Miley Cyrus date Liam Hemsworth? Their romance lasted over a decade, and it was a rollercoaster of love, split, and reunion that drew national attention. They met in 2009 while shooting “The Last Song,” were engaged in 2012, married in 2018, then announced their divorce in August 2019.

From their early beginnings until their abrupt parting, the couple’s love story has been both charming and traumatic. Miley Cyrus’ freshly debuted TikTok series “Used to Be Young” provides viewers with much more insight into their lengthy relationship, which she previously discussed.

Miley Cyrus and Liam Hemsworth’s relationship has piqued the interest of fans and the media alike. Their adventure lasted a decade and was filled with highs and lows. Before coming to an end, the couple’s youthful love bloomed on a movie set and suffered actual and metaphorical flames. Miley’s most recent TikTok series, “Used to Be Young,” digs deep into their relationship, giving information on their Malibu home, which was devastated by flames in 2018.

Early Beginnings and Intermittent Relationship

Miley was 16 when they first met, and Liam was 19. Love bloomed swiftly on the set of “The Last Song.” However, the relationship was not always plain sailing; there were frequent breakups and reconciliations. Miley also had a moment of reflection in her TikTok series, looking back at the Malibu home they lost in the 2018 flames.

Marriage and Quick Divorce

They chose to be married in 2018. However, less than a year into their marriage, the pair announced their divorce. It was a quick end to a romance that had lasted almost a decade. Miley and Liam claimed “irreconcilable differences” as the cause for their breakup, although they said that they would always have a strong bond.

Liam Hemsworth’s perspective

Liam freely addressed their relationship in a 2015 Men’s Journal interview. He claimed that they had been dating for five years at the time and intimated that he wasn’t ready for marriage at the time. Liam’s point of view complicates the topic, “How long was Miley Cyrus with Liam Hemsworth?” It also explains why their relationship saw so many ups and downs.

Miley Cyrus’ Music Video Teases

Miley Cyrus’ music video for “Flowers” sparked controversy. Fans believe the film includes Easter eggs concerning her relationship with Liam, tracking its various stages. While nothing has been verified, the music video adds another depth to their almost ten-year romance.

Chemistry and Reminiscences

During the tryouts for “The Last Song,” Miley said she had “undeniable chemistry” with Liam. This chemistry seems to have remained constant throughout their relationship’s ups and downs. Miley has often mentioned publicly how thankful she is for the time they spent together, regardless of the result.

“Used to Be Young” TikTok Video Series

Miley’s TikTok series “Used to Be Young” just documented their 10-year marriage. The show provides viewers an inside look at her life, particularly her relationship with Liam. While the series evoked emotions and memories, it also provided an opportunity for Miley to give her perspective on their experience.

Conclusion

Miley Cyrus and Liam Hemsworth’s friendship was undeniably important in both of their lives. Despite the ups and downs, the duo’s decade-long adventure left an indelible impression on them. Both have moved on, but the mystery of how long Miley Cyrus was with Liam Hemsworth continues to fascinate fans and the media.
