Kylie Jenner slammed for 'weird' treatment of daughter Stormi, 5, as fans spot 'sad' pattern in her

KYLIE Jenner has been slammed for the different ways she has shown her young children on social media.

The Kardashians star recently shared a photo with her one-year-old son, Aire, where she was seen hiding his face from the camera.

Kylie, 26, shares her son and daughter Stormi, five, with her ex Travis Scott, 32.

The reality star's photo showed her enjoying some pool time with her son as they sat on an outdoor lounge chair, dressed in bathing suits.

Kylie had her arm around Aire's head, which was covered by a hat, as she put her face slightly in front of his.

Her hair flew in front of his face, concealing his identity and maintaining his privacy in the public post.
