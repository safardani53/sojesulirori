Jennifer Aniston Rocks a Long Blazer, Harem Pants at Broadway Show

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Her tried and true is still flawless!

Jennifer Aniston may love to stick to her all-black outfits, but can you blame her when she looks so damn good? Case in point: her head-to-toe ebony look while attending Larry David's Broadway comedy, Fish in the Dark, on Tuesday, April 28, in NYC.

The Friends alum, 46, oozed Big Apple elegance in a short-sleeved black top, beaded harem-style pants, and a long crisp blazer. She accessorized the look with a small black purse, ankle-strap sandals, and a golden necklace.

Adding just a touch of her classic California style to the ensemble, Aniston (who dined at Omar's with friends after the show) rocked sun-kissed skin. Modeling a berry-stained lip, she styled her honey-hued tresses straight and slightly tousled.

Musing on her glorious locks, Aniston, who's been engaged to Justin Theroux since 2012, dished to Fashionista on what's next for that mane—especially if her longtime stylist, Chris McMillan, has anything to say about it. "Chris has that thing where he's like Edward Scissorhands," she joked. "He just sees hair and he wants to cut it! He would love for me to go… he wants to just cut it all off, kind of like a pixie, which I will never do."

That said, she doesn't always ignore her Living Proof business partner's expertise. "He always likes to give it a good two inches every year, just a healthy cut," Aniston explained, which she can handle. "He's also suggested going back to dark brown. We play with different things. I do listen to him sometimes."
