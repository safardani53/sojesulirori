September 2024 · 3 minute read

SISTER Wives star Kody Brown has been slammed by fans for the treatment of his daughter during the most recent episode.

Episode 4 of Sister Wives Season 18 aired on Sunday night, showing Kody, 54, celebrating Christmas with wife Robyn Brown, 44, then-wife Meri Brown, 52, and their children.

The scene first showed Janelle Brown, 54, spending the holidays alone with her children, including 18-year-old Savanah, at a vacation rental.

While Janelle noted how the holiday was a "relief," she also said she felt a "sadness" due to the fact that Kody was "not showing up for his kids."

Savanah labeled the day as "quiet" but "kind of pleasant" as she and her brothers were shown opening their stockings while sitting on the couch.

Janelle said she had not heard from any other family members that day, to which Kody explained he didn't "see the point" in trying to make their situation work for the holiday.

Kody continued to call Janelle "out of mind" and "out of sight" for him, saying he was "blocking everything else" out for that day unless it was about his "loyal" wife and children.

With Kody not reaching out to Savanah and the others, many angry viewers have called out the TLC star for his all-time low behavior.

FEELING FOR SAVANAH

After the episode aired, one person took their thoughts to Reddit, writing: "I feel so bad for that girl !!! She really doesn't deserve to be abandoned the way she was."

They also blamed Kody's wife Robyn for not telling him to call his daughter on Christmas.
