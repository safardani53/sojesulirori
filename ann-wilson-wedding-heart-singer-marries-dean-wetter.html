June 2024 · 2 minute read

Ann Wilson Wedding: Heart Singer Marries Dean Wetter

Beneath a canopy of flowers, Ann and Dean became husband and wife in front of their closest family members and best friends. The magical ceremony took place in the backyard of Ann’s manager, Carol Peters.

Ann explains her unique love story with Dean. “Dean and I first met in the ’80s and went on a date where we both got smashed and I tried to seduce him. Like the gentleman he is, he declined. Years passed, we got back in touch after we had both grown up, and Pow! – It worked! Did it ever!” Ann told People. 

OMG. Adorbs!

As for Ann’s bridal style, the Rock & Roll Hall of Fame inductee donned a bright purple gown by BFF designer Anthony Sartino, pairing it with Giuseppe Zanotti shoes and showing off her 2-carat white diamond set in a platinum band, and rose-cut white diamonds set in white-gold briolette earrings by Rose de France.

The newlyweds, who prove true love can find you no matter what age you are, are seriously on cloud nine after becoming Mr. and Mrs. “He is my friend, my angel, my husband,” the bride shares. “I am truly happy.”

We’re happy for you too, Ann!
