Who Are the Celebrity Jeopardy Contestants Tonight? Details

September 2024 · 3 minute read

What's going down on tonight's episode of 'Celebrity Jeopardy'? Here are the contestants along with the charities they are supporting.

Looking to watch your favorite celebs battle it out in the ultimate trivia game — you know, Jeopardy! — for the chance to win money for a charity of their choice? If so, then Celebrity Jeopardy is the show for you!

Hosted by Mayim Bialik, Celebrity Jeopardy introduces a new group of three celebrities each week who are competing for the chance to earn money for their chosen nonprofit.

Who are the celebrity contestants competing on Celebrity Jeopardy tonight? And which charities are they playing for? Read on for all the known details!

'And Just Like That' star Cynthia Nixon competes for SAG-AFTRA Foundation.

Cynthia Nixon plans to put her winnings from tonight's Celebrity Jeopardy episode toward the SAG-AFTRA Foundation, a nonprofit "dedicated to providing the most comprehensive, educational and state-of-the-art resources to SAG-AFTRA members."

Cynthia is best known for her role as Miranda Hobbes on Sex and the City and its sequel series And Just Like That. She has also been featured in a number of films from Amadeus to Igby Goes Down, and has stepped into the political arena as well.

Cedric the Entertainer plays for SSM Health Foundation.

Originally from St. Louis, comedian-actor Cedric the Entertainer plays for his hometown as he competes for SSM Health Foundation – St. Louis, an organization that works "[raise] awareness and financial support for various medical outreach and advancement programs throughout our region."

Cedric is best known as one of the Original Kings of Comedy and has starred in TV and film projects including The Steve Harvey Show, The Soul Man, and Ice Age.

Heather McMahan enters the ring for the City of Refuge.

Atlanta native Heather McMahan is placing her earnings toward City of Refuge, a "faith-based environment that helps individuals and families transition out of crisis" in Atlanta.

Between her sold-out stand-up tours, her Netflix special, and appearances in flicks like Love Hard and The Trap, Heather has become one of those most in-demand comedians.

Where can you stream episodes of 'Celebrity Jeopardy' after they air live?

If you missed out on the famous contestants competing in the Celebrity Jeopardy final tonight, don't you worry! After the episode airs live, you can stream it on Hulu, ABC's sister streaming service.

Who will reign victorious on the season finale of Celebrity Jeopardy? Tune in on Wednesday at 8 p.m. EST on ABC or as previously stated, stream the following day on Hulu!
