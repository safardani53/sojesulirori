Dairy Queen customers are raving over new blizzard flavor with a twist saying they are seeing 'heave

April 2024 · 3 minute read

DAIRY Queen has launched a new sugary treat with a cookie twist – and fans are going wild over the taste.

The fast food chain has updated its blizzard menu to include the cake batter cookie dough flavor.

Dairy Queen told The U.S. Sun that it would launch June 26 at select U.S. stores.

At a local location, the new cake batter flavor sells in the range of $4.46 and $6.50.

The cost will depend on the size, which could be had in mini, small, medium, and large options.

The new flavor features confetti cookie dough pieces, and sprinkles, as well as a mix of both cake batter and Dairy Queen's traditional soft-serve ice cream.

Keep in mind that this is not to be confused with an old flavor that has been discontinued for about a decade.

That's the confetti cake variety, which was first introduced in 2012.

While it was brought back briefly in 2014 – it hasn't been on the menu since.

It came with cake pieces, soft-serve ice cream, and confetti frosting.

The cake batter flavor will be a part of Dairy Queen's July Blizzard of the Month offering – but will only be available for a limited time.

While it's unclear for how long the new flavor will stay on the menu, fans who have gotten their hands on it are raving about it.

One Twitter user said: "I DID get a Dairy Queen cake batter cookie dough blizzard and I have seen heaven."

Another on the social media platform wrote: "The new Cake Batter Cookie Dough Blizzard at Dairy Queen is incredible."

And a third said that the new flavor is "too good" and that they "want another one."

MORE DAIRY QUEEN CHANGES

The cookie dough blizzard flavor is just one new item Dairy Queen has launched for the summer.

Other new options include the Oreo brookie blizzard and peanut butter puppy chow blizzard.

When it comes to drinks, Dairy Queen has launched the summertime sunset.

It is an ice blend that features mango and strawberry flavors.

To save money, make sure you sign up for the fast food chain's loyalty program known as DQ Rewards.

There you can review offers under the "deals" section.

For example, Dairy Queen is offering a free soft drink after purchasing a six-piece chicken strip basket for a limited time.

Keep in mind that offers and prices can vary by location.
